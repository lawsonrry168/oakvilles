Your Journey

Consultation Process & Fees

From booking to treatment, every step is clear and transparent. We believe patients deserve to fully understand the consultation process and costs involved.

Steps

Full Consultation Process

I
Step 1
WhatsApp / Phone Booking

Send a WhatsApp message or call to share your main concern and preferred date and time. We confirm within one business day and remind you of first-visit essentials (please let us know if you need to pause other medications).

II
Step 2
Arrive & Register

Please arrive five minutes early to complete a basic health form. Bring any Western medical reports, medication records or lab results if available — this helps Dr. Ng assess your situation more comprehensively.

III
Step 3
Four-Method Assessment

Dr. Ng uses the traditional four diagnostic methods (inspection, listening and smelling, inquiry, palpation): observing complexion and tongue, listening to voice and breath, detailed symptom and history inquiry, and pulse reading to understand organ and blood-qi status. First visits allow ample time for a thorough understanding of your health.

IV
Step 4
Personalised Treatment Plan

Based on your pattern diagnosis, Dr. Ng clearly explains your constitutional issues and treatment direction, selects the most suitable approach (acupuncture, herbal medicine, tuina or a combination), and outlines the expected number of sessions and timeframe. Individual results vary.

V
Step 5
Treatment & Dispensing

Acupuncture or tuina treatment (approximately 20–40 minutes), with herbal prescription as needed. Dr. Ng explains decoction methods, timing and precautions for any herbs prescribed.

VI
Ongoing Care
Follow-Up & Adjustments

At follow-up visits, Dr. Ng assesses progress and adjusts acupuncture points or herbal formulas as needed. Follow-up frequency varies by condition; typically once or twice weekly is recommended.
