Personalised Herbal Prescriptions

Dr. Ng Hau Jun, Registered TCM 003769, prescribes one formula per person based on four-diagnosis assessment, with flexible adjustments at follow-up to support constitutional balance.

The One-Prescription Approach

TCM emphasises that the same symptom may need different formulas for different constitutions. Dr. Ng uses four-diagnosis assessment to identify cold-heat and deficiency-excess patterns, then adjusts herbs at follow-up based on your response.

Formulas are available as decocted herbs or convenient granules. Commonly used for dermatology, internal medicine, gynaecology and pain — often alongside acupuncture for inside-out care.
