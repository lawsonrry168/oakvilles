Why Patients Choose Oakville Wellness

Private Setting

Separate consultation and treatment rooms with controlled waiting numbers — suited to busy professionals and first-time visitors who value discretion.

II

Clear Care Flow

Reception → consultation and pulse assessment → herbal prescription or acupuncture/tuina. The dispensary stocks granule formulas for convenient dispensing.

Central Access

Approx. 3–5 minutes from Sheung Wan MTR Exit E2. We recommend booking via WhatsApp 6734 9532 to minimise waiting time.

Space Overview

Waiting area — Calm and comfortable; intake forms available on arrival. TCM texts on display create a serene atmosphere.

Consultation room — Dr. Ng conducts thorough four-diagnosis assessments without rushing. First visits include a clear explanation of your pattern and care direction.

Herbal dispensary — Granule formulas or decoction guidance prepared according to your prescription, with dosing and dietary notes.

Acupuncture / tuina room — Dedicated treatment beds with single-use needles and standard hygiene protocols. Your comfort and consent are confirmed before each procedure.

Reception — Booking confirmation, fee summary and follow-up scheduling. Questions welcome at any stage.
