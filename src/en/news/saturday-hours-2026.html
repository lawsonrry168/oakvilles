Saturday clinic hours

From 2026, Saturday hours remain 10:30–17:30 — convenient for weekday workers booking weekend consultations.

Published by: Oakville Wellness 5 January 2026 Announcement

Thank you for your continued trust in Oakville Wellness · Dr. Ng Hau Jun. In response to growing appointment demand and to better serve patients who cannot visit on weekdays, we are pleased to confirm our 2026 clinic schedule below.

2026 opening hours

Monday to Friday: 10:30 a.m. – 7:30 p.m.
Saturday: 10:30 a.m. – 5:30 p.m.
Sunday and public holidays: Closed

Saturday sessions are especially popular with working professionals. As Saturday slots fill quickly, we recommend booking one to two days ahead via WhatsApp or phone to secure your preferred time.

How to book

You may book through any of the following:

WhatsApp: 6734 9532 (recommended — fast response)
Phone: 2881 8182
Walk-in: Room 602, 6/F, Kam On Building, 176A–176F Queen's Road Central

First-time patients should allow approximately 45–60 minutes for a full four-method assessment. Follow-up visits typically take around 30 minutes. If you require herbal dispensing, please let us know during your visit so we can arrange collection time.

Getting here

The clinic is in the heart of Central, close to MTR Central and Sheung Wan stations, with bus and tram routes along Queen's Road Central. For detailed directions, see our Contact page or the Central clinic guide.

Other notices

During Typhoon Signal No. 8 or above, or when the Black Rainstorm Warning is in force, the clinic will suspend service. We will contact booked patients as soon as possible after signals are lowered to reschedule. Special arrangements for Lunar New Year, Christmas, Easter, and other long holidays will be announced on this page.

Saturday appointments include the same specialties as weekdays — dermatology, gynaecology, pain management, and internal medicine. New patients, follow-ups, and acupuncture are all available. See our Process & fees page for details.

Questions? Message us on WhatsApp anytime. We look forward to supporting your health.
