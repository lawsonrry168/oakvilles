Central Practice

Central Hong Kong Clinic

Oakville Wellness is located in the heart of Central, led by Registered Chinese Medicine Practitioner Dr. Ng Hau Jun. We specialise in skin conditions, gynaecology, pain management and internal medicine — offering private, professional TCM care for patients across Hong Kong Island and Kowloon.

Convenience & Privacy in Central

Oakville Wellness is located at Unit 602, 6/F, Kam On Building, 176A–176F Queen's Road Central, Central, Hong Kong — approx. 3–5 minutes from Sheung Wan MTR Exit E2 and major bus routes. Office workers from Central, Admiralty and Sheung Wan can reach us on foot. Situated on an upper floor of a commercial building, away from street-level noise, the clinic separates waiting and consultation areas to protect patient privacy.

Central is Hong Kong's financial and professional hub, where residents and office workers often face high stress, poor sleep and irregular eating — areas where TCM may offer meaningful support. Dr. Ng Hau Jun understands urban constitutions and can arrange follow-up schedules that suit busy lifestyles.

The clinic blends traditional TCM atmosphere with modern comfort, making each visit a brief pause for body and mind. See our clinic environment page for photos of the space.

Dr. Ng Hau Jun & Areas of Focus

Dr. Ng Hau Jun is a Registered Chinese Medicine Practitioner with the Chinese Medicine Council of Hong Kong (003769), holding degrees from Guangzhou University of Chinese Medicine and Xiamen University in acupuncture. With twenty-five years of practice in Hong Kong and a Google Maps rating of 5.0 / 5 (3 verified reviews), he has spent over a decade specialising in eczema, acne, gynaecological health, pain management and internal medicine.

His approach — internal and external treatment together, pattern-based diagnosis, addressing root and branch — begins with a thorough first consultation of approximately thirty to forty-five minutes, followed by a personalised plan that may include herbal medicine, acupuncture, moxibustion and cupping.

Whether you work or live in Central, or travel specifically to see us, you receive the same standard of care. Common concerns include eczema, acne, insomnia, fertility support, neck pain and sciatica. WhatsApp us to ask whether TCM may be suitable for your situation.

Booking & Consultation Hours

Consultation hours: Monday to Friday 10:30–19:30, Saturday 10:30–17:30. To book, WhatsApp 6734 9532 or call 2881 8182. We recommend booking in advance to minimise waiting time.

Fees are transparent — first visit, follow-up, acupuncture and herbal medicine are itemised separately. See our process & fees page and price list for details. We accept cash, FPS, Octopus and other payment methods.

For first visits, please arrive five to ten minutes early and bring any previous medical reports if available. We look forward to supporting your wellbeing in Central.

Whether you come from Central, Admiralty, Sheung Wan or elsewhere on Hong Kong Island, Kam On Building is easy to find. Cafés and restaurants nearby make it convenient to rest after your appointment; the building has lift access for elderly patients and those with limited mobility.
